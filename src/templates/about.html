Art Style Guesser

by Land Belenky, 2018

An ensemble of five Convolutional Neural Networks and a Support Vector Classifier to identify the artistic style of paintings.
Using images from WikiArt.org scraped with BeautifulSoup, convolutional neural networks in Keras, Tensorflow and Theano and a Flask application on Amazon Web services.
Based in part on work by Nitin Viswanathan and Francois Chollet.
Many thanks to my instructors and classmates at Galavanize Data Science Immersive, Denver, Colorado.
